# 📖 Overview

**TransitOps** is a modern fleet management platform designed to simplify transport operations for logistics companies.

The platform enables organizations to:

- 🚚 Manage vehicles
- 👨‍✈️ Track drivers
- 🗺️ Plan and monitor trips
- ⛽ Record fuel consumption
- 🔧 Schedule maintenance
- 💰 Monitor operational expenses
- 📊 View real-time dashboards and analytics

# 📂 Modules

### 🚚 Fleet Management

Manage fleet information including vehicle status, capacity, and availability.

---

### 👨‍✈️ Driver Management

Maintain driver profiles, licenses, assignments, and availability.

---

### 🛣️ Trip Management

Create, assign, monitor, and complete transport trips.

---

### 🔧 Maintenance

Track vehicle servicing, repairs, and maintenance schedules.

---

### ⛽ Fuel Management

Monitor fuel usage, costs, and mileage.

---

### 💰 Expense Management

Record operational expenses such as tolls, fuel, repairs, and parking.

---
